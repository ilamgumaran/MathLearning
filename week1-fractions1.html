Part 1: What is a Fraction?

A fraction is a way of saying "a piece of something." It has two parts:

3 8
  1. Bottom Number (Denominator): How many pieces make the whole? (The Name)
  2. Top Number (Numerator): How many pieces do you have? (The Count)

Part 2: How to Compare Fractions

Method A: Common Numerator

The Rule: If the Top Numbers are the SAME, the fraction with the smaller bottom number is BIGGER.

Think about a cake. Sharing with 3 people gives you big slices. Sharing with 10 people gives you tiny crumbs!

Use this when the numbers are totally different. We Cross Multiply!
