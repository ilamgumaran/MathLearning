1. The Basics Review

Top Number (Numerator): How many pieces you have.

Bottom Number (Denominator): How many pieces make a whole.

Example: 34 means 3 pieces out of 4.

2. Transforming Fractions

A. Improper to Mixed (The Pizza Box Method)

Convert 134 into a Mixed Number.

Ask: How many times does 4 fit into 13?

  • It fits 3 times (because 4 × 3 = 12).
  • How many are left over? 13 - 12 = 1 slice left.
  • The bottom number stays 4.

Answer: 3 14

B. Mixed to Improper (The MAD Method)

Convert 2 35 into an Improper Fraction.

M
Multiply: Whole number (2) × Bottom (5) = 10
A
Add: Take the 10 and add the Top (3) = 13
D
Denominator: Stays the same = 5

Answer: 135
